Mission Statement

Our mission here at The Velvet Velvet is to celebrate the creativity
and passion behind architectural and graphic design. We highlight
the beauty of the design process, from early sketches to final
forms, so the community can better appreciate how art comments
on, connects with, and shapes the world around us.
